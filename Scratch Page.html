The Mavs' Starting Five After The Draft





How will the Dallas Mavericks win the championship in 2026?

Here are the steps the Dallas Mavericks need to take to win the NBA championship this next season.

  1. Get and stay healthy
    • Kyrie Irving tore his ACL at the end of the last season. He is eying a comeback in January. Once he is back, he needs to stay healthy.
    • Anthony Davis and Dereck Lively need to come back 100% at the start of next season and stay that way

  2. Get Cooper Flagg comfortable
    • After all of the hype, the Mavs coaching staff needs to calm Cooper Flagg's nerves and get him integrated.
    • More experienced players, like Kyrie, AD, and Klay all need to welcome Flagg and build off his strengths. This shouldn't be too challenging since Flagg played with AD last summer in the Olympics.

  3. Make the playoffs
    • This might seem obvious, but the Mavs need to be in a position to at least make the play-in.
    • Though they won't be fully healthy until January, if they can play well enough until then, they will be in a position to figure things out before the playoffs start in April.
    • If they do this, they can hit the postseason in stride like they did 2 years ago when they made it to the Finals. If they do this this year, however, they will win the championship.
